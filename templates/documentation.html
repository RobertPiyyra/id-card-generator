How to Generate ID Cards

1

Create an Account

Click on the "Register" button in the top navigation and fill out the registration form with your details including name, email, password, and school information.

2

Login to Your Account

Use your registered email and password to login to the system. Make sure to select the correct school name associated with your account.

3

Fill Student Information

Enter the required student details including full name, father's name, class/grade, date of birth, complete address, and phone number. All fields are mandatory for ID card generation.

4

Upload Student Photo

Upload a clear, recent photograph of the student in JPG or PNG format (max 2MB). The system will automatically crop it to the required dimensions and apply professional enhancements.

5

Select Template

Choose from available ID card templates designed for your institution. Each template can be customized with different fonts, colors, and layouts to match your school's branding.

6

Open The Visual Editor

After selecting a template, open the Visual Editor to control the actual card layout. You can work on the front side and, for double-sided templates, switch separately to the back side.

Front / Back editing Grid and guides Layers panel
7

Customize The Layout In The Editor

The Visual Editor now works as a full layout workspace for template design. You can adjust labels, values, colons, photo and QR blocks, and also add your own custom objects to the card.

Field Controls

  • Move labels, values, and colons separately
  • Hide label or value without empty gaps
  • Change color, font size, and grow behavior
  • Fine-tune colon visibility & alignment

Workspace Tools

  • Zoom, fit canvas, undo, redo
  • Snap movement & optional editor grid
  • Bleed and safe-area guides
  • Arrow-key nudging for precision

Custom Objects

  • Add text, rectangles, circles, lines, logos
  • Rename objects for layer management
  • Set exact position, opacity, stroke
  • Rotate supported objects, save angle

Arrangement Tools

  • Bring to front, send to back, lock/unlock
  • Align left, right, top, bottom, center
  • Distribute multiple objects evenly
  • Duplicate, copy, paste, select all
Ctrl/Cmd + D duplicate Ctrl/Cmd + C / V copy-paste Ctrl/Cmd + L lock/unlock Arrow keys nudge
8

Generate & Download

Click the generate button to create your professional ID card. All saved Visual Editor settings are applied in preview, generation, edit/regenerate, bulk generation, and PDF rendering.
